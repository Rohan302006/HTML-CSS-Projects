Creamy Garlic Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g Noodles
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 Cup Cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsely (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water
  2. saute garlic in olive oil for 1-2 min.
  3. Add Cream and stir over low heat
  4. Drain pasta and mix into sauce
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and persly. Serve hot!
